## Tax Regulations

### Federal Tax

**Primary Authority**: 26 USC (Internal Revenue Code)

**EITC (Earned Income Tax Credit)**: `26 USC ยง 32`
- **2025 Maximum Credits**:
  - No children: $617
  - 1 child: $4,213
  - 2 children: $6,960
  - 3+ children: $8,046
- **Eligibility**: Earned income, age 25-64 (no children), valid SSN, U.S. residency
- **IRS Publication**: Pub 596

**CTC (Child Tax Credit)**: `26 USC ยง 24`
- **2025 Maximum Credit**: $2,200 per qualifying child (indexed for inflation)
- **Refundable Portion**: $1,700 (15% of earnings above $2,500 threshold)
- **Phase-out**: Begins at $400,000 (married joint), $200,000 (all others)
- **Recent Change**: P.L. 119-21 (July 2025) made TCJA expansions permanent, increased to $2,200
- **IRS Publication**: Pub 972

**VITA Program**: IRS Publication 4012 (VITA/TCE Volunteer Resource Guide)
- Confidentiality: Pub 4299, Pub 1075
- Scope limitations defined by IRS SPEC program
